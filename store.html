What does "Emotions Sit" refer to? A concept borrowed from Eastern philosophy, it is said that our emotions "live in the basement" and although it is natural for them to come up now and then, we shouldn't invite them to stay for too long.

Track #4 on Jennie's debut album refers to this concept as her lyrics explain, "Emotions sit here with me, but I won't, (as one yogi put it) "invite them for dinner, just for tea."
